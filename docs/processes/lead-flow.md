## Steps

| # | Step | Location | Entity |
|---|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Visitor arrives at landing page | Hero (U03) | U01 |
| 2 | Reads value proposition (headline + description) | Hero (U03) | U03 |
| 3 | Scrolls through sections to build trust | About, Programs, Trainers, Testimonials | U04-U08 |
| 4 | Views pricing plans to evaluate fit | Membership (U06) | U06, U13 |
| 5 | Clicks primary CTA ("Join Now") or secondary CTA ("Book Free Trial") | Hero / Header / Footer | U03, U02, U10 |
| 6 | Reaches Contact section | Contact (U09) | U09 |
| 7 | Option A: Calls/emails directly via info cards | Info cards | U09 |
| 8 | Option B: Fills out contact form | Contact form (U16) | U16 |
| 9 | Form shows "Sent вң“" confirmation | Contact form (U16) | U16 |

---

## CTA Touchpoints (where user can convert)

1. **Header CTA** вҖ” persistent button in Navbar (U02)
2. **Hero primary CTA** вҖ” "Join Now" in hero section (U03)
3. **Hero secondary CTA** вҖ” "Book Free Trial" in hero section (U03)
4. **Announcement bar CTA** вҖ” top banner link (U17)
5. **Footer CTA** вҖ” bottom section CTA button (U10)
6. **Contact form** вҖ” "Send Message" submit (U16)
7. **Phone link** вҖ” direct call from info card (U09)
8. **Email link** вҖ” direct email from info card (U09)

---

## Current Limitations

- вқҢ No backend вҖ” form submit is frontend-only (shows "Sent вң“", resets after 2s)
- вқҢ No analytics tracking on CTA clicks
- вқҢ No WhatsApp CTA integration yet
- вқҢ No email capture / newsletter signup
- вқҢ Phone number is placeholder (+91 XXXXX XXXXX)
